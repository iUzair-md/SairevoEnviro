Company Overview

SaiRevo Enviro Private Limited is a dedicated environmental consultancy firm committed to driving sustainable solutions for businesses and industries. Established in Gurugram (Haryana) in 2023, we bring over two years of expertise in providing comprehensive environmental compliance and sustainability consulting services to both public and private sector clients. Our goal is to support organizations in navigating environmental regulations, ensuring compliance, and integrating eco-friendly practices into their operations.

We are a purpose-driven startup committed to advancing environmental sustainability through cutting-edge solutions and responsible resource management. Our mission is to help businesses and industries reduce their environmental impact while fostering a more circular, regenerative economy.

Beyond consultancy, we actively promote circular economy principles by partnering with industries engaged in hazardous waste co-processing and sustainable waste management solutions. As an associate channel partner of cement industries and authorized recyclers, we help facilitate responsible waste disposal, resource recovery, and eco-conscious material repurposing, reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainable industrial practices.

At SaiRevo Enviro, we believe in transforming environmental challenges into opportunities for sustainability, innovation, and long-term ecological balance. Through our integrated environmental solutions, we empower businesses to adopt responsible environmental practices, reduce their carbon footprint, and contribute to a greener, more sustainable future.

With a holistic approach to integrated environmental services, we collaborate closely with businesses to design and implement solutions that drive long-term environmental impact. Our goal is to help industries transition toward greener operations, embrace sustainable innovations, and build a future where economic growth and ecological well-being go hand in hand.

At SaiRevo Enviro Pvt. Ltd., we specialize in facilitating regulatory approvals and environmental clearances from the State Pollution Control Board (SPCB). Our professional expertise extends to obtaining Consent to Establish (CTE), Consent to Operate (CTO), and various authorizations under key environmental laws, including:

Hazardous Waste Management Rules

Biomedical Waste Rules

E-Waste (Management) Rules

Plastic Waste Management Rules
